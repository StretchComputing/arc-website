What is Arc

What: A revolutionary mobile payment platform which improves the payment process top to bottom for both the merchant and consumer. From restaurants and landlords to doctor's offices and utilities providers, Arc is the smart payment solution for any service provider.

How: Research. Based on the pains of payment processes, we built an innovative, efficient, and secure platform that links the merchants that you buy from to you and your mobile phone.

Why: Fast. Secure. Efficient. Need to split the bill by cost? Percentage? Individual items? No problem, Arc is loaded with key features to make your payments easier.
Need more reasons? Arc enables you to leave real-time feedback for the merchant and we reward you with Arc Rewards for each transaction. For every 1000 points you earn, we'll throw $10 in your bank account or towards credit on your next bill.

When: Launching 2013 in Chicago!

Merchants for Merchants

Save Money. Arc's strategic partnership with Dwolla empowers us to provide merchants the ability to accept ACH payments as easily as credit or cash. And, an ACH payment through Arc comes with a flat surcharge of $0.75 –about 76% less than that of the average credit card provider.

Save Face. Real-time consumer feedback enables merchants to take any corrective or affirmative action before the consumer has reached the door. Consumers are prompted and encouraged to leave feedback as soon as the payment is complete.

Save Time. From bill splitting to quick and secure credit card acceptance, Arc's special features are designed to save your business time and make the payment process easier.

Arc gives restaurant customers the power to split their own bill, so servers can spend less time at the POS system and more time at their tables. And, when a consumer pays with a credit card, Arc sends the information directly to your POS, so your team is free to get back to work.
